Breakfast

Pancakes

Ingredients
  1. 1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  2. 1 ½ tablespoons white sugar
  3. 1 tablespoon baking powder
  4. 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  5. ¾ teaspoon salt
  6. 1 ⅓ cups milk
  7. 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  8. ¾ teaspoon vanilla extract
  9. 1 teaspoon vegetable oil

Directions

  • Whisk together flour, sugar, bakingpowder, cinnamon, and salt in a large bowl.
  • Whisk in milk, beaten egg, 3 tablespoons vegetable oil, and vanilla until only a few lumps remain.
  • Let batter stand for 5 full minutes for extra fluffiness.

Lunch

Italian Pasta

Ingredients
  1. 2 LBS. of San Marzano tomatoes (or plum tomatoes)
  2. 14 OZ. of eggplant
  3. 12 OZ. of short pasta such as sedani or ziti
  4. 2/3 CUP of ricotta salata cheese, grated basil
  5. 1/2 onion
  6. extra-virgin olive oil
  7. peanut oil
  8. salt
Directions
  • Roughly chop the onion and sauté it in a saucepan with 3 Tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil.
  • Chop the tomatoes into large pieces. Tie a dozen basil leaves together and place in the saucepan with the onion.
  • After 1 minute, add tomatoes and salt. Cook for 25 minutes over medium-low heat. Remove the basil a few minutes before turning off the heat. Pass the tomato sauce through a food mill.
  • Heat oil to 350°F. Wash the eggplant and cut them into 1/4" thick slices; fry in hot oil for 1-2 minutes.
  • Cook the pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente. Drain.
  • Toss the drained pasta in the tomato sauce. Drizzle with some extra-virgin olive oil. Top with fried eggplant and ricotta salata and serve.
  • Optional: preheat the oven to 210°F. Roast cherry tomatoes with peel from half of a lemon for 50 minutes. Top the pasta with the roasted tomatoes and serve.

Dinner

Quick Apricot Chicken

Ingredients
  1. 1/2 cup apricot preserves
  2. 2 tablespoons reduced-sodium soy sauce
  3. 1 tablespoon chicken broth or sherry
  4. 1 tablespoon canola oil
  5. 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  6. 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  7. 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  8. 1 pound boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into strips
  9. 1 medium green pepper, chopped
  10. 1/2 cup salted cashews
  11. Hot cooked rice
  12. Crushed red pepper flakes, optional
Directions
  • In a shallow microwave-safe dish, combine the first seven ingredients; stir in chicken. Cover and microwave on high for 3 minutes, stirring once.
  • Add green pepper and cashews. Cover and microwave on high for 2-4 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink, stirring once. Let stand for 3 minutes. Serve with rice and, if desired, sprinkle with red pepper flakes.
